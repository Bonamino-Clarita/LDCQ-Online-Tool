Lucid Dream Control Questionnaire (LDCQ)

About this Questionnaire

A lucid dream is a dream during which you are aware of being in a dream and may involve control of the dream content and events. The Lucid Dream Control Questionnaire (LDCQ) is a scientifically developed assessment tool designed to evaluate different aspects of volitional dream control. By completing this questionnaire, you will receive a personalized Dream Control Profile that summarizes your perceived dream control abilities across seven dream control domains: own cognition, own emotion, own body, own movement, dream characters, dream environment, and dream physics. The questionnaire contains 36 items and takes 10-15 minutes to complete.

How to Respond

For each statement, indicate how often you are typically able to deliberately and successfully achieve the described form of dream control. Use the following scale:
Never = 0; Rarely = 1; Sometimes = 2; Often = 3; Always = 4
If you have never attempted a particular type of dream control, mark it as “N.A.” (Not Applicable).
Please answer the questions based on your typical lucid dreaming experiences rather than a single dream. There are no right or wrong answers, so please respond as honestly as possible.

Important

Your rating should reflect how often you successfully achieve a particular form of dream control when you intentionally attempt it. For example, selecting "Rarely" (score = 1) means that when you attempt this type of dream control, you rarely succeed. It does not mean that you rarely attempt it. Similarly, selecting "N.A." indicates that you never attempted that form of dream control, it does not imply that you would be unable to perform it.

Understanding Your Score and Dream Control Profile

The LDCQ provides two complementary scores for each domain:
  • Dream Control Score: The Dream Control Score reflects your perceived ability to successfully control a particular aspect of your lucid dreams. Scores range from 0 to 1 (0 = no perceived control; 1 = complete perceived control). Higher scores indicate greater perceived dream control ability within that domain.
  • Dream Engagement Score: The Dream Engagement Score reflects the breadth of dream control behaviours that you have attempted within a domain. Scores range from 0 to 1 (0 = no engagement; 1 = complete engagement). Higher engagement scores indicated greater breadth and lower scores poorer breadth.

Dream Control Profile

After completing the questionnaire, your results will be displayed as multidimensional Dream Control Profile, allowing you to visualize patterns of dream control and engagement across domains. Rather than treating lucid dream control as a single ability, this profile highlights its strengths, limitations, and areas of engagement across multiple aspects of your lucid dream experience. You will also have the option to download your Dream Control Profile and scores.
